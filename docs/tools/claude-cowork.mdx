**Claude just went from chatbot to co-worker. This changes everything.**

Claude CoWork lets AI work directly inside your actual business tools — Excel, PowerPoint, Google Docs, Chrome, and more. Instead of copying and pasting between a chat window and your applications, Claude now sits inside them and does the work.

---

## Why This Matters

Until now, every AI tool worked the same way:

1. You open a chat window
2. You copy something from your spreadsheet / document / browser
3. You paste it into the chat
4. AI gives you a response
5. You copy the response back into your tool

That copy-paste loop is slow, clunky, and breaks the moment your task gets complex.

**Claude CoWork removes the loop entirely.** Claude reads your files, understands your context, and takes action — all inside the tools you already use.

Think of it as the difference between texting someone instructions vs. having them sit next to you and just do it.

## What Can It Actually Do?

### Excel & Spreadsheets

Claude reads your entire workbook — not just one cell or one sheet. It sees formulas, dependencies, and structure.

**Real example:** You have 50 receipt photos from a conference. Claude extracts every line item, organises them into a spreadsheet, adds confidence scores for each entry, generates pivot tables by category, and creates summary charts. What would take an afternoon takes minutes.

**For your team:** Think financial data across multiple sources — sales figures, budget tracking, performance reports. Claude could consolidate everything into one workbook, flag anomalies, and build the summary tables you actually need — without anyone manually copying numbers between sheets.

---

### PowerPoint & Presentations

Claude reads your slide master — your templates, fonts, colour schemes, layouts. Then it works within them.

**Real example:** You have a 40-slide deck that's grown messy over months of edits. Claude restructures it: identifies the key metrics, eliminates redundant slides, ensures consistent formatting, and produces an executive-ready version that matches your brand.

**For your team:** Board reports, investor updates, quarterly reviews — instead of spending hours formatting slides, Claude builds them using your existing templates. You review and refine rather than build from scratch.

---

### Chrome Browser

This is where it gets powerful. Claude operates within your actual browser session — meaning it can access anything you're logged into.

**What that means:**
- Read and act on your emails in Gmail
- Pull data from internal dashboards
- Navigate CRM systems like Salesforce
- Extract competitive intelligence from websites
- Fill in forms and update records

---

### Google Docs & Notion

Claude reads and writes directly in your documents. No more copying paragraphs into a chat window.

Draft reports, update briefings, or restructure long documents — all happening inside the actual document rather than in a separate AI window.

## The Features That Matter Most

### Scheduled Tasks

Set Claude to run workflows automatically — daily, weekly, or monthly.

**Example:** Every Monday morning, Claude checks three competitor websites, compiles what's changed, and drops a summary in your inbox before you've finished your coffee.

This is the kind of work that's important but never urgent — so it never gets done. Automation fixes that.

---

### Shortcuts & Workflows

Record a task once. Claude learns the steps and repeats them whenever you need.

You show Claude what to do (it watches your screen and listens to your narration), and from then on, it can replicate that workflow independently. Like training a new team member, except they remember perfectly every time.

---

### Parallel Processing

Claude can work on multiple tasks simultaneously across different browser tabs.

Instead of doing tasks one-by-one — research this, then update that, then compile this — Claude handles them all at once. The time savings compound fast.

## Why This Is a Big Deal for Business

### The Real Problem With AI Today

Most businesses are stuck in what you might call the "chat window era" of AI. Someone on the team opens ChatGPT, asks it to rewrite an email, copies the result, and pastes it into Outlook. That's useful, but it's like having a brilliant consultant who can only communicate through Post-it notes.

Claude CoWork breaks through that wall. Instead of AI being a tool you visit, it becomes a tool that works alongside you — inside the same applications, with access to the same files, understanding the same context.

### What Changes

| Before (Chat Window AI) | After (Claude CoWork) |
|---|---|
| Copy data into AI, get response, paste back | AI reads and writes directly in your spreadsheet |
| Describe your slides to AI, manually rebuild | AI reads your templates and builds within them |
| Screenshot a dashboard, ask AI to analyse | AI navigates the dashboard itself |
| Run one task at a time | Multiple tasks running in parallel |
| Every task needs manual setup | Recurring tasks run on schedule |

### The Compound Effect

The value isn't in any single feature — it's in removing friction from dozens of small tasks every day. When AI can read your files, understand your context, and take action without you supervising every step, the time savings add up dramatically.

A conservative estimate: if CoWork saves 30 minutes per day of copy-paste busywork and manual formatting, that's **10+ hours per month** freed up for the work that actually requires human judgement.

## Common Questions

### "Is this safe for sensitive business data?"
Claude CoWork runs locally on your machine. Your files aren't uploaded to the cloud for processing — Claude reads them directly from your file system. That said, always follow your organisation's data policies and check with IT before connecting AI to sensitive systems.

### "Does it replace my existing tools?"
No. It works inside them. You still use Excel, PowerPoint, Chrome, and everything else. Claude just operates within those tools instead of in a separate chat window.

### "How is this different from ChatGPT?"
ChatGPT is a chat window. You type, it responds. Claude CoWork is an agent that takes action inside your applications. It's the difference between asking someone for advice and having someone do the work.

### "What if it makes a mistake?"
You maintain oversight. Claude shows you what it's doing, and you can review, approve, or adjust. Think of it as a very capable assistant who checks in with you rather than a fully autonomous robot.
